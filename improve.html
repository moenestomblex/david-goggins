-Books-

You Can’t Hurt Me by David Goggins is a powerful book about pushing past your limits and taking control of your life through discipline and mental toughness. Goggins shares his painful childhood, failures, and struggles, showing how he transformed himself through relentless hard work and a “never quit” mindset.
Dopamine Nation by Dr. Anna Lembke explores how modern life constantly overstimulates our brains with pleasure—from social media to food to drugs—making us more anxious, restless, and unhappy. The book explains how dopamine drives our cravings and how too much pleasure leads to pain and addiction.
How to Win Friends and Influence People by Dale Carnegie is a classic guide to building strong relationships through empathy, respect, and genuine communication. The book teaches that people respond best when they feel valued, listened to, and appreciated. By focusing on others—remembering names, avoiding criticism, showing honest interest.
